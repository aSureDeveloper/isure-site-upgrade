1) Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how iSure Myanmar (“iSure”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit or use isuremyanmar.com and related services (the “Services”).

“Personal information” means information that identifies you or can reasonably be linked to you (e.g., name, phone number, email, address).

2) Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact details: name, phone number, email address, delivery address, and similar details.
  • Account information (if applicable): username, account preferences, and related profile details.
  • Order and transaction information: items purchased, order history, payment confirmation, delivery details, and support requests.
  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, and timestamps (often collected via cookies or analytics tools).
  • Communications: messages you send us via forms, chat, email, social media, or phone.
  • Optional marketing data: campaign responses, promo code usage, and subscription preferences.
We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as health or biometric data) through this website unless you choose to provide it.

3) How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • provide and operate the Services, including processing orders and delivering products;
  • communicate with you about orders, support, updates, and important notices;
  • verify identity and prevent fraud, misuse, or unauthorized access;
  • improve our website, products, and customer experience (e.g., analytics, troubleshooting);
  • send marketing messages (only where permitted and based on your preferences/consent);
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

4) Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: payment processors, delivery partners, hosting providers, analytics tools, and customer support tools.
  • Business partners (if applicable): for joint promotions or fulfillment, only as needed and subject to appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal and safety: where required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud.
  • Business changes: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

We do not sell your personal information as a business model.

5) Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, measure performance, and understand usage.

  • Essential cookies: required for site functionality (e.g., security, session management).
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

7) Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including providing Services, complying with legal requirements, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements.

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

To make a request, contact us using the details in Section 12. We may need to verify your identity before responding.
